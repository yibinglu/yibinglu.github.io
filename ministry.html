NEW DEPARTMENT: Society for the Promotion of Elfish Welfare

The Ministry of Magic is happy to annouce the opening of a brand new department in the ministry: Society for the Promotion of Elfish Welfare (SPEW). SPEW will deal and regulate all issues and regulations regarding house-elves. Anyone who wishes to have a house-elf or currently has one must be registered or else they may be removed. Abuse and mis-treatment will be reported and dealt with appropriatly by the head of department, Miss Hermione Granger. This department is still short a few staff members so please do not hesitate to send in your resume to our office by owl and we will be in contact with you shortly.

50th Anniversary of defeating You-Know-Who

50 years ago on this exact day, the most powerful dark wizard the world had ever seen was defeated by the one and only Harry Potter. Every year, we celebrate the peaceful world we now live in but we also mourn the loss of those that lost their lives fighting for our safety.
